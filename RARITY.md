## How rarity works

Every Sweetardio is generated from layered **traits** grouped into categories
(Background, Character, Eyes, etc.). A trait's rarity is simply how few NFTs
share it — **the lower the percentage, the rarer the trait.** An NFT's overall
rank is driven by its rarest traits.

Two things make a Sweetardio special:

1. **Optional layers** — not every NFT has Arms, Footwear, or a Sticker, so
   having one (or a rare combination) stands out.
2. **Secret Rarez** — a tiny chase tier of full-art specials.

### Reading the table
- **Universal (≈99.8%):** nearly every NFT has these — rarity here comes from
  the *specific value* (e.g. a rare background colour), not from having the
  category at all.
- **Optional layers (12–18%):** most NFTs do **not** have these, so any Arms,
  Footwear, or Sticker is already a minority trait.
- **Secret Rarez (0.20%):** only **9 of 4,444**. The rarest pieces in the
  collection.
